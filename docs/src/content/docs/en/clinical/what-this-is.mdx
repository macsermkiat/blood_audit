## In one paragraph

blood_audit reviews inpatient adult red blood cell (RBC) transfusions at
Chulalongkorn Hospital, after they are complete. It reads HOSxP exports
from the hospital information system. For each transfused unit, it
records one of five stored classifications: `APPROPRIATE`,
`INAPPROPRIATE`, `NEEDS_REVIEW`, `INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE`, or
`POTENTIALLY_INAPPROPRIATE`. The decision follows the KCMH internal
guideline PR 17.2 (Procedure Revision 17.2) and the AABB 2023
(Association for the Advancement of Blood & Biotherapies) standard,
using a 3-tier hemoglobin (Hb) policy with cohort-specific thresholds
and an acute-blood-loss override.

Rows that the deterministic classifier routes to the LLM (Tier 3,
Hb > 10 g/dL) go through six fail-closed quote-grounding layers.
"LLM_REVIEW" is a routing concept inside the pipeline; it is not a
stored classification. A failed grounding becomes
`final_classification = NEEDS_REVIEW` with `review_reason` set, so a
human reviewer can adjudicate.

## What it is not

- **Not Software as a Medical Device.** Nothing in this codebase influences
  a transfusion order at the point of care.
- **Not real-time.** No subscriber to the audit stream emits clinician
  alerts. No POC dashboard pushes recommendations.
- **Not a single-physician scorecard.** Per-physician views exist for the
  reviewing clinician's own audit (own-view layout); they are not a
  quality scorecard for management review.

If anyone asks for real-time CDS hooks, decline. That would move the
project into SaMD territory and change everything: regulatory scope,
review processes, deployment risk profile.

## What it produces

A monthly audit dataset for the QI committee. From this dataset, six
report sections are derived:

- Hospital-trend summary.
- Ward-scorecard.
- Physician own-view (per reviewer).
- Indication breakdown.
- Cohort breakdown.
- Health-state breakdown.

The report schema lives in [`docs/report-schema.md`](https://github.com/macsermkiat/blood_audit/blob/main/docs/report-schema.md)
and is summarized at [Report output](/blood_audit/en/clinical/report-output/).

## Anti-hallucination guarantees

For rows that the deterministic classifier routes to `LLM_REVIEW`
(Tier 3, Hb > 10 g/dL), the LLM's clinical reasoning is gated by
[six fail-closed quote-grounding layers](/blood_audit/en/developers/quote-grounding/).
A failed grounding routes the row to `hallucination_suspect`, not to a
result row.
